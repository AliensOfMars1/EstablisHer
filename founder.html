Meet Our Founder — SANDRA .A. ALEXANDRA

  EstablisHER was born out of real-life experiences — moments of silent struggles, uncertainty, and growth. Our founder once stood exactly where many girls do today: confused, overwhelmed, and without someone to talk to.

  She made decisions she wished she could change. But those moments shaped her mission — to become the big sister she never had. Through EstablisHER, she creates a space where every girl can feel seen, heard, and valued.

  My message is simple: You are not alone. You are enough. And your story is just getting started. ✨

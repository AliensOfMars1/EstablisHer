What Is EstablisHER?

EstablisHER is a girls-focused initiative that empowers Senior and Junior High School girls in Ghana to find themselves, be themselves, and become the best versions of themselves.

We create a safe space, offer mentorship, and equip girls with the knowledge, tools, and confidence to make informed decisions.

What Do We Do?

  • Visit schools to hold open discussions with girls
  • Talk about friendships, peer pressure, self-worth
  • Create safe spaces for honest conversations

Why It Matters

Many girls face silent struggles—feeling alone, confused, and misunderstood. EstablisHER offers sisterhood, honest conversations, and support to remind them: You matter. You’re enough. You’re not alone.

Why I Started EstablisHER

I once felt lost too—unsure of myself, my choices, and my emotions. If I had someone who truly understood back then, I could have become my best self sooner. Now, I want to be that person for other girls. A big sister. A guide. A reminder that their story isn’t over—they can rise, grow, and shine.

Vision Statement

To build a generation of confident, self-aware, and empowered girls who boldly take up space in the world.

Mission Statement

To support school girls in Ghana through mentorship and honest guidance, helping them grow emotionally and make better life choices.

Our Promise

No judgment. No pressure. Just real talk, real growth, and real sisterhood. We walk with you, grow with you, and support you.

Our Approach

  • Host girl-to-girl discussions
  • Share personal stories and lessons
  • Answer anonymous questions
  • Offer mentorship & emotional check-ins
  • Include fun, self-awareness activities

Core Values

  • Authenticity - We keep it real.
  • Empowerment - We uplift and inspire.
  • Sisterhood - We grow stronger together.
  • Growth - Every girl has the right to rise.
  • Compassion - We lead with understanding.
